Meet The Show

The Artists Behind New B @ The Z

New Bedford greats Caliph, Hendrick Hernandez-Resto, and Siahlaw perform for the first time on the stage that has loomed large for them growing up in New Bedford. The seasoned artists of the game unite to demonstrate that working together can truly amplify the genre. Backed by DJ Fresco and The Seshn Band, the trio offer an epic performance that’s not to be missed by fans of hip-hop or supporters of the community.

Caliph

Featured Artist
Instagram Apple Music Spotify

Caliph (pronounced Cuh-Leaf) is a Grammy winning artist blending hip hop, Afro, R&B, and world music into bold storytelling. Born in Dakar, Senegal and raised in New Bedford, MA, his music reflects his journey as a Black Muslim immigrant and explores themes of identity, resilience, and healing. After appearing on the Grammy winning album American Dreamers: Voices of Hope, Music of Freedom, he released Immigrant of the Year, a 10 track project chronicling struggle, growth, and self empowerment. Independent in every sense, Caliph writes, produces, designs, codes, and markets his work while building interactive digital experiences that connect his music directly with fans.

Resto

Featured Artist
Instagram Apple Music Spotify

Hendrick Hernandez-Resto is a Puerto Rican born interdisciplinary artist and Berklee College of Music trained audio engineer based in New Bedford, MA. A true DIY creator, he writes, produces, records, mixes, and distributes his own music while collaborating with organizations such as the Zeiterion Performing Arts Center, New Bedford Creative, and the New Bedford Historical Society. As a founding member of Sleepless In New England, his genre blending sound mixing boom bap, Caribbean influence, trap, pop, and jazz has helped expand hip hop’s presence in the arts and community spaces across the region.
